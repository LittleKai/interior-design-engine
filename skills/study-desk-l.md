# Study Desk L

## When to use
- User asks for ban hoc, ban lam viec chu L, or compact study corner.

## Recipe
1. Add `l-desk-return` as the main desk surface.
2. Add `open-bookshelf` above or beside the desk.
3. Add `upper-2door` for closed overhead storage.
4. Keep desk height around 75 cm.

## Common pitfalls
- Leave legroom below the desktop.
- Do not fill all lower space with cabinets unless requested.
